About Beluga

Learn about the history of Beluga, meet the core team, and check out the ever-growing community resources.

History

Originally conceived by students from the EPITECH, Beluga try to create the future of the web game frameworks using Haxe.

Beluga was created in 2013 by @masadow and is currently in constant developpement.

Core team

Beluga is maintained by the founding team and a small group of invaluable core contributors, with the massive support and involvement of our community.
